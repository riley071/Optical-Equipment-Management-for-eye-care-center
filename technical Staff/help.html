Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log in?

To log in, go to the login page and enter your username and password in the provided fields. Click on the "Log In" button to access your account.

How can I change my password?

To change your password, go to your account settings or profile page. Look for the "Change Password" option and follow the prompts to enter your current password and set a new one.

How do I use the search bar?

To use the search bar, simply type your search query into the search field and press Enter or click on the search icon. The system will then display the search results based on your query.

How can I print a report?

To print a report, navigate to the section containing the report you want to print. Look for the "Print" or "Download" option, which may be located in the toolbar or menu. Select the desired report format and follow the prompts to print the report.

How do I add new equipment?

To add new equipment, navigate to the Equipment section and click on the "Add Equipment" button. Fill out the required information and submit the form.

How can I update equipment details?

To update equipment details, go to the Equipment section and find the equipment you want to update. Click on the "Edit" or "Update" button next to the equipment entry, make the necessary changes, and save the updates.

What should I do if I encounter an issue?

If you encounter any issues or have questions that are not addressed in this FAQ, please contact our support team for assistance. You can find the contact information on the About page.

How do I add new equipment?

To add new equipment, navigate to the Equipment section and click on the "Add Equipment" button. Fill out the required information and submit the form.

How do I delete equipment?

To delete equipment, navigate to the Equipment section and find the equipment you want to delete. Click on the "Delete" button next to the equipment entry, confirm the deletion when prompted, and the equipment will be removed from the system.

Can I export equipment data?

Yes, you can export equipment data. Navigate to the Equipment section and look for the "Export" or "Download" option. Select the desired format (e.g., CSV, Excel) and follow the prompts to download the equipment data.
